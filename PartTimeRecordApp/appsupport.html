PartTimeRecord version:1.0

This is an app that records part-time work. It is very convenient to record part-time job information, and can record the start and end time of part-time work, so that you can directly view the working time of each part-time job. You can also record your part-time income so that you can view the current year's income statement through the report.


1. Part-time work record:

Record part-time job information, such as job content, working time range, work location, job compensation, contacts, and phone number.


2. work timing:

Record the working time of each part-time job, record the start time each time you start work, and record the end time at the end, the system will automatically calculate the hours, so that you can easily check your working time;


3. Revenue record: Record the income of your part-time job. You only need to input the amount of part-time income, income date, remarks, and you can also view the income statement for each month of the current year;
